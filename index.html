School Achievments

SOCCER STARS
The TSRS Aravali boys soccer teams participated in the soccer championship held at The Heritage School Gurgaon from 29th August to 31st August 2014. 16 teams participated in the tournament from various schools of Gurgaon and Delhi . The Shri Ram School Aravali won the championship in both categories:The Under-12 years team won the final against The Sardar Palel Vidyalaya Delhi (Score 6-1). The Under-14 years team won the final against DPS Sushant Lok School Gurgaon (Score 1-0)
